# BotForge Electronics - Store Policies

Welcome to BotForge Electronics! Here are our customer service policies:

1. **Return Window**: We accept returns on all electronics, gadgets, and accessories within 30 days of the original delivery date.
2. **Item Condition**: To be eligible for a full refund, items must be in brand-new, unopened condition with all original seals intact. Opened electronics will incur a 15% restocking fee.
3. **Refund Processing**: Once our warehouse receives your return, refunds will be credited back to your original payment method within 3 to 5 business days.
4. **Defective Items**: If you receive a defective or damaged product (e.g., cracked screen, won't turn on), please contact us within 48 hours for a free replacement. We will cover the return shipping costs.
5. **Shipping Costs**: For standard returns (buyer's remorse), the customer is responsible for the return shipping fee.
6. **Support Contact**: For technical support, warranty claims, or return authorizations, please email our support team at `support@botforge-electronics.com` or ask our AI assistant for help!
